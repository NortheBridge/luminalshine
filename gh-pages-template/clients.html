Moonlight compatibility

Stream anywhere Moonlight runs.

LuminalShine speaks the standard Moonlight streaming protocol. Any official or community Moonlight client can pair with a LuminalShine host — or skip the client entirely and stream straight to your browser via the built-in WebRTC endpoint.

Built into LuminalShine

WebRTC Browser Client

Browse to /webrtc on your LuminalShine host from Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox — no separate app to install. Useful on locked-down devices and for quick sessions from a guest machine.

First-party Browser

Twilight (LG webOS)

NortheBridge’s sister project for LG webOS OLED TVs. Brings low-latency PC gaming directly to the living room, available on the webOS App Store.
